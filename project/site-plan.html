Site Name

The name for the site, "Exercise Hub" was chosen because it will immediately be apparent to visitors what the topic of the site is. It is a brief and informative name that will not confuse visitors.

Logo

I designed my logo, and it perfectly matches the main colors!

Purpose and Audience

The purpose of Exercise Hub is to provide an easy-to-use platform for individuals to track their workouts, visualize their fitness progress, and stay motivated on their health journey. This site is designed for fitness enthusiasts, beginners, and anyone interested in maintaining or improving their physical activity levels.

Description of the dynamic elements

Responsive Navigation Bar: For mobile users, a hamburger menu icon will open and close a navigation menu. JavaScript will handle the menu's visibility and smooth transition effects.
